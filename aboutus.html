About us...

JK clothing was launched in 2014. We are a growing couture clothing brand providing customers with a street style premium quality aesthetic, at affordable prices. We offer a wide range of products for both men and women and even offer custom design t-shirts thats can be configured, customized and personalized by you. We aim to provide an excellent shopping experience to all our customers both online and in our stores.

Locations

We currently have 15 stores across the UK as well as many concessions. Our uk stores include high street retailers such as Debenhams, House of Fraser, New Look etc. As well as on other online stores such as ASOS.
